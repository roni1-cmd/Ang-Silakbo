Core Team

Editor-in-Chief

Leads the entire publication process

The Editor-in-Chief is responsible for:

  • Making final decisions on content and layout
  • Leading the editorial team
  • Setting the publication's vision and direction
  • Representing the publication at events

Associate Editor

Assists in managing the publication

The Associate Editor works closely with the Editor-in-Chief to:

  • Edit content for quality and accuracy
  • Coordinate with different departments
  • Assist in planning editorial calendars
  • Provide guidance to writers

Managing Editor

Oversees day-to-day operations

The Managing Editor is responsible for:

  • Managing deadlines and workflow
  • Coordinating between departments
  • Ensuring timely publication
  • Resolving operational issues

Production Head

Manages the production process

The Production Head is responsible for:

  • Coordinating between editorial and printing teams
  • Overseeing layout and design
  • Managing production timelines
  • Ensuring quality control

Assistant Production Head

Supports the production process

The Assistant Production Head works to:

  • Support the Production Head in daily tasks
  • Handle specific aspects of production
  • Coordinate with design team
  • Troubleshoot production issues

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I join Ang Silakbo?

To join Ang Silakbo, you can apply through our online application form or attend one of our recruitment events. We typically recruit at the beginning of each semester. Check our social media pages for announcements about recruitment periods.

Do I need prior experience to join?

While prior experience is beneficial, it's not always required. We value passion, dedication, and willingness to learn. We provide training for new members to help them develop the necessary skills for their roles.

How much time commitment is required?

Time commitment varies by position. Writers typically spend 5-10 hours per week, while editorial positions may require 10-15 hours. We understand that academics come first and work to accommodate busy schedules.

Can I contribute without being a full-time member?

Yes! We welcome guest contributors. If you have a story idea or would like to submit content without the commitment of regular membership, contact us at angsilakbouclm@gmail.com.

About Ang Silakbo

Ang Silakbo is the premier student publication committed to excellence in journalism and media. We provide a platform for student voices and cover important issues affecting our community.

Our team consists of dedicated students passionate about journalism, photography, design, and digital media. We strive to uphold the highest standards of journalistic integrity while providing valuable experience to our members.

Founded in 2021, Ang Silakbo has grown to become an essential source of information and creative expression for our campus community.

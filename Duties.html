Shaping the Future of Media

Discover the roles that drive our passion for impactful journalism at Ang Silakbo

Core Team

Editor-in-Chief

Leads the entire publication process, making final decisions on content and layout

Associate Editor

Assists in managing the publication, editing content, and coordinating with departments

Managing Editor

Oversees day-to-day operations, manages deadlines, and ensures smooth workflow

Production Head

Manages the production process, coordinates between editorial and printing teams

Assistant Production Head

Supports the production process, handles specific aspects of production

Writers

Feature Writers

Craft compelling narratives on a wide range of topics, conducting interviews and gathering information to present our readers with engaging, thought-provoking content.

Editorial Writers

Shape our publication's stance on important issues, analyzing current events and crafting opinion pieces that stimulate discussion and encourage critical thinking.

Local News Writers

Cover events and issues that directly impact our readers' daily lives, gathering information from local sources and writing timely and accurate news articles.

Sports Writers

Bring the excitement of athletic competitions to our pages, covering local and national sports events, writing game recaps, and conducting interviews with athletes and coaches.

Entertainment News Writers

Keep readers informed about the vibrant world of arts and culture, covering industry news and events, writing reviews, and interviewing celebrities and industry professionals.

Literary Writer

Craft original literary works such as poetry and short stories, contribute to the publication's creative writing section, and organize literary events and workshops.

Managing Team

Circulation Manager

Oversee the distribution of our publication, manage subscriptions and readership data, and develop strategies to increase our circulation.

Assistant Circulation Manager

Work closely with the Circulation Manager to ensure smooth distribution operations, handle customer inquiries, and maintain accurate circulation records.

Production Team

Photojournalists

Capture compelling images to complement our news stories, document events visually, and collaborate with writers to enhance storytelling.

Videographers

Shoot video footage for news stories and features, conduct video interviews, and operate camera equipment and lighting.

Video Editors

Edit video content, add graphics, music, and special effects, ensuring all video content meets our publication's high standards.

Broadcasters

Present news and information on-camera or via audio, conduct interviews for broadcast segments, and collaborate with the production team for live broadcasts.

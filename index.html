Our Commitments

Our Mission and Vision

The primary mission of Ang Silakbo is to serve as a platform for students to voice their opinions, showcase their talents, and report on various events and achievements within the university. By providing a space for creative expression, the publication aims to inform the public about the actions of the student body, policies, current issues, students welfare, programs, official activities, and the Senior High School departments achievements.

Content and Coverage

Ang Silakbo covers a diverse range of topics, including local news, sports, features, and entertainment. For instance, it has reported on UCLM's participation and successes in events like the Sepak Takraw competitions, where the university secured multiple podium finishes, highlighting the athletic prowess of its students.

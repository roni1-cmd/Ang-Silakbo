Volitronix AI Assistant - Privacy Notice

Important: Before interacting with Volitronix, our AI assistant, please be aware that your conversations may be monitored and reviewed by the developer for quality assurance and improvement purposes.

What information is collected?

When you interact with Volitronix, we collect and store:

  • The content of your conversations with the AI
  • Metadata such as timestamps, session duration, and interaction patterns
  • Technical information about your device and browser
  • Any feedback you provide about the AI's responses

How we use this information

The information collected is used to:

  • Improve Volitronix's responses and capabilities
  • Train our AI models to better understand user queries
  • Identify and fix technical issues or bugs
  • Develop new features based on user interaction patterns
  • Ensure the AI adheres to our ethical guidelines and content policies

Who has access to your conversations?

Your conversations may be accessed by:

  • Our AI development team
  • Quality assurance specialists
  • Authorized researchers working on AI improvement

Data retention and security

We implement strict security measures to protect your data and retain conversation logs for up to 12 months. After this period, data is anonymized for long-term research purposes.

By proceeding to use Volitronix, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this privacy notice. For complete details about how we handle your data, please refer to our full Terms of Service.

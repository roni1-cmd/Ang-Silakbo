The Archives

Dive into our extensive collection of past issues, articles, and features. Our archives represent a rich tapestry of stories, perspectives, and journalistic excellence spanning years of dedicated reporting and storytelling.

Each publication captures a moment in our school's history, preserving the voices, achievements, and challenges of our community. From investigative reports to creative expressions, our archives serve as both historical record and inspiration.

  • In-depth features on student achievements and campus events
  • Compelling photostories documenting school life and community activities
  • Thought-provoking articles on educational trends and youth issues
  • Breaking news coverage of important developments at UCLM-SHS
  • Creative works showcasing student talent in writing and visual arts

Connect With Us

Join our vibrant community of readers, writers, and thinkers. Connect with ANG SILAKBO through our social media channels, events, and interactive platforms to stay updated on the latest stories and engage in meaningful conversations.

We believe in the power of connection and dialogue. Our publication serves as a bridge between students, faculty, and the wider community, fostering understanding and collaboration through shared stories and experiences.

  • Engage with our editorial team and fellow readers through comments and discussions
  • Participate in workshops, forums, and events organized by ANG SILAKBO
  • Share your feedback, suggestions, and story ideas directly with our team
  • Follow our social media channels for behind-the-scenes content and updates
  • Join our newsletter community for exclusive content and early access

Share Your Voice

Submit Your Work

Have a story to tell? A perspective to share? ANG SILAKBO welcomes submissions from passionate writers, photographers, and artists who want to contribute to our platform and reach a wider audience.

We believe that every student has a unique voice and perspective worth sharing. Our publication provides a platform for diverse expressions, from journalistic reporting to creative works, all contributing to the rich tapestry of our school community.

  • News articles, feature stories, opinion pieces, and investigative reports
  • Creative writing including poetry, short stories, and personal essays
  • Photography portfolios, photo essays, and documentary projects
  • Illustrations, digital art, comics, and graphic design work
  • Multimedia content including videos, podcasts, and interactive stories

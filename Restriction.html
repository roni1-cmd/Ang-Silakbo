Membership Restriction Notice

You cannot join ANG SILAKBO because you are currently a member of another sub-organization.

Membership Application Status: Restricted

Your application has been noted but cannot be processed due to existing organizational commitments.

Thank you for your interest in becoming a member of ANG SILAKBO. We appreciate your enthusiasm for contributing to our literary publication. However, we've identified that you are currently an active member of another sub-organization.

Why This Restriction Exists

This policy has been implemented based on years of experience and feedback from both organization advisors and students. We've found that allowing simultaneous memberships often results in burnout, missed deadlines, and decreased quality of contributions.

Here's why:

  • Conflicting commitments: Being part of multiple organizations often leads to scheduling conflicts, especially during peak periods like events, competitions, and publication deadlines.
  • Dedication requirements: Ang Silakbo requires dedicated members who can fully commit to our publication schedule, attend regular meetings, and meet strict deadlines.
  • Focus on current commitment: We encourage you to focus on your current organizational commitment and consider applying after your term ends.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our membership restriction policy.

Can I still submit my written work?

Yes! We welcome literary submissions from all students, regardless of membership status. You can submit your poems, short stories, essays, and other creative works through our online submission portal for consideration in upcoming issues.

What if my role isn't time-intensive?

While we understand that involvement levels vary, our policy applies to all official members of sub-organizations. However, you may request a special exemption review if you believe your circumstances warrant consideration.

When can I apply to join?

You can apply immediately after your term with your current sub-organization ends. We hold recruitment drives at the beginning of each semester (August and January). If your term ends mid-semester, you may be eligible for our mid-year intake.

Can I participate without full membership?

You can participate as a contributor by submitting work, attending our public workshops and literary events, or volunteering for specific one-time projects that don't require ongoing commitment.

Can I appeal this decision?

Yes, appeals can be submitted to our faculty advisor with supporting documentation explaining your unique circumstances. While exceptions are rare, we do consider appeals on a case-by-case basis.

Next Steps & Recommendations

We understand this may be disappointing news, but we have several recommendations for how you can proceed:

  • Complete your current term: Focus on making the most of your experience with your current sub-organization.
  • Stay connected: Subscribe to our newsletter and follow our social media to stay updated on Ang Silakbo's activities.
  • Contribute as a non-member: Submit your literary works for consideration in our upcoming publications.
  • Attend public events: Many of our workshops, readings, and literary discussions are open to the broader student body.
  • Plan ahead: If joining Ang Silakbo remains a priority for you, consider planning your organizational commitments accordingly for next semester.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to get a response?
We typically respond to inquiries within 24-48 hours during business days. High priority inquiries may receive faster responses. If your inquiry is urgent, please select "High" priority when submitting your form.
What types of inquiries can I submit?
You can submit general questions, technical support requests, partnership inquiries, feedback, and any other questions related to Ang Silakbo. If your inquiry doesn't fit into the provided categories, please select "Other" and provide details in your message.
Is my information kept confidential?
Yes, all information submitted through this form is kept strictly confidential and is only used to address your inquiry. We adhere to privacy best practices and will never share your personal information with third parties without your consent.
Can I track the status of my inquiry?
Yes, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can use this to check the status of your inquiry on our website. Additionally, you can view your recent inquiries in the "Recent Inquiries" section once you've submitted a form.

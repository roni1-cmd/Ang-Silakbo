Our Journey

2021

Ang Silakbo was founded by the University of Cebu Lapu-Lapu and Mandaue Senior High School SSC with a vision to empower student voices.

A.Y. 2023-2024

Started its operations to provide a platform for student voices, creativity, and engagement within the university community.

A.Y. 2024-2025

Ang Silakbo participated in its first competition at the Division Schools Press Conference in Mandaue City, where it won four awards, marking a significant achievement for the publication.

Present

Ang Silakbo is still expanding, continuing to grow its presence and influence within the UCLM community while further developing its programs and initiatives.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Ang Silakbo Forums

What is Ang Silakbo Forums?
Ang Silakbo Forums serves as the official student publication of the University of Cebu Lapu-Lapu and Mandaue (UCLM) Senior High School department. Established in 2021, this publication aims to inform and engage the student body by covering a range of topics, including student activities, institutional policies, current events, student welfare, programs, and the department's achievements. The publication maintains an active presence on social media, particularly on Facebook, where it shares updates and information relevant to the UCLM community.
Is my information kept confidential?
We take privacy and confidentiality very seriously. All personal information, including names, contact details, and any other sensitive data, is handled with the highest level of security and discretion. Additionally, all posts, messages, and communications shared within our platform are strictly confidential and will not be disclosed to any third parties without explicit consent. We are committed to ensuring a safe and secure environment for all users, where privacy is respected and protected at all times.
How can I join a support group?
You can browse through the available groups, explore their descriptions, and choose the ones that best align with your needs, experiences, or personal goals. Whether you're looking for emotional support, shared experiences, or valuable resources, you can join the groups that resonate with you and connect with like-minded individuals in a safe and welcoming environment.
Are the mindfulness exercises guided?
Yes, we provide a variety of mindfulness exercises designed to suit different preferences and lifestyles. You can choose from guided sessions, where an instructor leads you through techniques such as deep breathing, meditation, and visualization, or opt for self-paced exercises that allow you to practice mindfulness at your own convenience. Whether you prefer structured guidance or the flexibility to explore mindfulness on your own, our offerings are designed to help you cultivate a sense of calm, focus, and overall well-being.

About Ang Silakbo Forums

Ang Silakbo Forums is a revolutionary platform dedicated to emotional well-being and personal growth. Our name, which means "The Outburst" in Filipino, represents our commitment to providing a safe space for individuals to express their emotions freely and authentically.

Our Mission

  • Express: Provide tools for articulating feelings without judgment.
  • Connect: Facilitate meaningful connections between individuals.
  • Explore: Offer resources for self-awareness and personal growth.

Quick Summary

This section provides a simplified overview of our terms but is not a substitute for the full terms below. We strongly encourage you to read the entire document carefully.

What You Need to Know

ANG SILAKBO is committed to providing a safe, respectful, and productive environment for all users. These guidelines outline our expectations, policies, and procedures to ensure a positive experience for everyone in our community.

Key Points

  • Must be registered to Google to use ANG SILAKBO Forums
  • No harmful or illegal content allowed
  • Respect user privacy and intellectual property rights
  • Regular account maintenance and security updates required
  • Comprehensive data protection measures in place
  • User content remains user-owned, with limited platform rights
  • Dispute resolution process available for conflicts

Important Warnings

  • Account termination for terms violations
  • Content monitoring and moderation in place
  • Disciplinary actions may be taken for serious breaches
  • No unauthorized data sharing or scraping
  • Mandatory security updates and practices
  • Service modifications may occur with notice
  • User responsible for maintaining account security

1. Definitions

Core Terms

  • Platform: ANG SILAKBO and all associated services, websites, and published properties
  • User: Any individual or entity accessing or using our services
  • Content: All forms of user-generated materials, including but not limited to text, images, videos, and audio
  • Service: Any feature, functionality, or offering provided by ANG SILAKBO
  • Data: Any information collected, stored, processed, or transmitted by the platform
  • Account: A user's registered profile and associated information on ANG SILAKBO
  • Terms: This Terms of Service document and any related policies

2. Account Management

Registration Process

  • Valid Google account required for account creation
  • Age verification process (must be 16 or older)
  • Acceptance of Terms of Service and Privacy Policy
  • Creation of a unique username and secure password (via Google)
  • Optional profile customization

Account Security

  • Two-factor authentication (2FA) mandatory for all accounts (via Google)
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Immediate notification of suspicious activities or unauthorized access attempts
  • Option to review login history and active sessions

Account Maintenance

  • Quarterly account activity reviews and security checks
  • Automated security scans for potential vulnerabilities
  • Annual account verification process to ensure up-to-date information
  • Regular password changes recommended (at least every 6 months)
  • Periodic review of connected applications and devices

Account Termination

  • Users may terminate their account at any time through Google account settings
  • ANG SILAKBO may terminate accounts and posts for violations of Terms of Service
  • 30-day grace period for data retrieval after account termination
  • Appeal process available for disputed terminations

  • Regular data backups with secure off-site storage

  • Continuous monitoring for potential data breaches
  • Annual third-party security audits
  • Data minimization practices to reduce risk

4. Content Guidelines

Acceptable Content

  • Educational materials and resources
  • Creative works (sports, technology, writing)
  • Professional content and portfolios
  • Community discussions and forums
  • Constructive feedback and reviews
  • Factual information and news
  • Personal blogs and journals
  • Tutorials and how-to guides

Prohibited Content

  • Illegal materials or promotion of illegal activities
  • Harmful or dangerous content
  • Hate speech or discriminatory content
  • Misleading information or fake news
  • Explicit or pornographic material
  • Copyrighted content without proper permission
  • Personal information of others without consent
  • Spam, phishing, or malicious content
  • Content that violates others' privacy or intellectual property rights

Content Moderation

ANG SILAKBO employs a combination of automated systems and human moderators to ensure compliance with our content guidelines. We reserve the right to remove any content that violates these guidelines without prior notice. Repeated violations may result in account suspension or termination.

  • Initial automated screening for obvious violations
  • User-driven reporting system for community moderation
  • Human review for complex or disputed cases
  • Appeals process for content removal decisions
  • Regular updates to moderation policies based on emerging trends and user feedback

5. Intellectual Property

Content Ownership and Licensing

Users retain ownership of their original content while granting ANG SILAKBO specific rights to use and display that content on the platform.

  • Users maintain copyright of their original content
  • ANG SILAKBO Forums receives a non-exclusive, worldwide, royalty-free license to use, reproduce, modify, and distribute user content solely for the purpose of operating and improving the platform
  • This license ends when the user deletes their content or account, except where:
    • The content has been shared with others who have not deleted it
    • ANG SILAKBO Forums has used the content for promotional purposes (with user consent)
  • Users are responsible for ensuring they have the necessary rights to post their content
  • ANG SILAKBO respects third-party intellectual property rights and expects users to do the same

ANG SILAKBO's Intellectual Property

The ANG SILAKBO platform, including its design, logos, software, and features, is protected by copyright, trademark. Users agree not to:

  • Copy, modify, or create derivative works of the ANG SILAKBO platform
  • Reverse engineer or attempt to extract the source code of our website
  • Use ANG SILAKBO's trademarks, logos, or branding without explicit permission
  • Remove, obscure, or alter any legal notices displayed in or along with our services

6. Platform Security

Security Measures

  • End-to-end encryption for all user communications
  • 24/7 security monitoring via webmaster
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Advanced threat detection systems
  • Secure data storage with multiple redundancies
  • Compliance with industry security standards

User Responsibilities

While ANG SILAKBO implements robust security measures, users play a crucial role in maintaining the security of their accounts and data. Users are expected to:

  • Use strong, unique passwords for their ANG SILAKBO account
  • Enable and use two-factor authentication
  • Keep their login credentials confidential
  • Log out from shared devices after use
  • Regularly update their devices and software
  • Report any suspicious activities or potential security issues
  • Avoid clicking on suspicious links or downloading unknown attachments

7. Service Modifications

Change Management

  • ANG SILAKBO reserves the right to modify, suspend, or discontinue any part of the service at any time
  • Users will be notified of changes via email and/or prominent notices on the platform
  • Continued use of ANG SILAKBO after changes constitutes acceptance of the modifications

User Feedback

We value user input in our service evolution. Users can provide feedback on changes through:

  • Our feedback forum accessible from the user dashboard
  • Quarterly user surveys
  • Beta testing programs for new features (opt-in basis)

Changes to Helpline Service

ANG SILAKBO reserves the right to modify, suspend, or discontinue the Helpline service at any time without prior notice. Users will be informed of any significant changes through our official communication channels.

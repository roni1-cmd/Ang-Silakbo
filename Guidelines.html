Submission Guidelines

Ang Silakbo welcomes submissions from both established and emerging writers. We are committed to showcasing diverse voices and innovative storytelling. Please read the following guidelines carefully before submitting your work:

  • All submissions must be original and previously unpublished works
  • Accepted file formats: .doc, .docx, or .pdf
  • Maximum word count: 5,000 words for prose, 100 lines for poetry
  • Include a brief bio (50-100 words) and contact information
  • Simultaneous submissions are allowed but please inform us immediately if accepted elsewhere
  • We accept submissions in English and Filipino languages
  • Please use standard manuscript formatting: double-spaced, 12-point font (Times New Roman or Arial)

Send your submissions to: submissions@angsilakbo.com

Genres We Accept

Short Fiction Poetry Creative Nonfiction Flash Fiction Experimental Writing Literary Criticism

Tips for a Successful Submission

  • Proofread your work carefully before submitting
  • Ensure your piece aligns with our journal's theme and style
  • Follow all formatting guidelines precisely
  • Include a compelling cover letter introducing yourself and your work
  • Be patient – our review process is thorough and may take some time

Editorial Process

At Ang Silakbo, we believe in a rigorous and fair editorial process. Here's what you can expect after submitting your work:

  • Initial screening by editorial team (1-2 days): Our team reviews all submissions to ensure they meet our basic criteria and guidelines.
  • Detailed review by genre editors (2-3 days): Submissions that pass the initial screening are then carefully evaluated by our genre-specific editors.
  • Final decision and feedback (5 days): Our editorial board makes the final selection and provides feedback to authors.
  • Revision process: If accepted, authors will work closely with an assigned editor to refine and polish their work for publication.

We strive to respond to all submissions within 5 days to 1 week. If there are high volume of submissions, we may not be able to provide detailed feedback for every piece.

Copyright Policy

At Ang Silakbo, we respect and protect the intellectual property rights of our contributors. Our copyright policy is designed to benefit both the authors and our publication:

  • Authors retain full copyright of their work
  • Ang Silakbo receives first publication rights
  • After publication, authors may republish their work elsewhere with acknowledgment to Ang Silakbo
  • All published works will be archived in our digital repository
  • We request a 90-day exclusivity period from the date of publication

For any copyright-related queries, please contact: copyright@angsilakbo.com

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I submit multiple pieces for consideration?

A: Yes, you may submit up to three pieces per issue. However, please send each piece in a separate email.

Q: How long does the review process take?

A: Typically, the entire review process takes 4-6 weeks from the submission deadline. However, this may vary depending on the volume of submissions we receive.

Q: Do you offer feedback on rejected submissions?

A: Due to the high volume of submissions, we are unable to provide detailed feedback on every piece. However, we do offer general comments on submissions that reach the final round of consideration.

Q: Is there a fee for submitting work to Ang Silakbo?

A: No, there is no fee for submitting your work to Ang Silakbo. We believe in providing equal opportunities for all writers, regardless of their financial situation.

Q: Do you accept translations?

A: Yes, we accept translations. However, you must have the rights to translate and submit the work. Please include information about the original work and author with your submission.

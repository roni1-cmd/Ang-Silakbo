The Voice of UCLM Students

The journey of Ang Silakbo, the voice of UCLM Senior High School

Our Beginning

Ang Silakbo is the official student publication of the University of Cebu Lapu-Lapu and Mandaue (UCLM) Senior High School Department. Established in Academic Year 2020-2021, Ang Silakbo was created to serve as a platform for amplifying student voices, showcasing creativity, and fostering meaningful engagement within the university community.

Our publication is guided by a mission to inform, inspire, and empower. We aim to highlight the achievements, initiatives, and unique stories of the UCLM Senior High School student body. From covering significant news and events to celebrating the talents of our athletes and creative minds, Ang Silakbo is dedicated to reflecting the vibrant culture and spirit of our university.

Mission & Vision

Our guiding principles and aspirations for the future

Our Mission

Ang Silakbo is committed to providing accurate, relevant, and timely information to the UCLM Senior High School community. We strive to be a platform that amplifies student voices, promotes critical thinking, and fosters a culture of excellence in journalism and creative expression.

We aim to document the vibrant life of our school, celebrate achievements, and address issues that matter to our community. Through our work, we seek to inspire, inform, and empower our readers while upholding the highest standards of journalistic integrity and creativity.

Our Vision

We envision Ang Silakbo as a leading student publication that serves as a beacon of truth, creativity, and unity for the UCLM Senior High School community and beyond. We aspire to be recognized for our excellence in journalism, innovative content, and meaningful impact on our readers.

Our vision is to cultivate a generation of critical thinkers, skilled communicators, and responsible citizens who value the power of words and the importance of diverse perspectives in shaping our shared future.

Our History

Our Journey

Key milestones in the evolution of Ang Silakbo

2021

Ang Silakbo, founded by the University of Cebu Lapu-Lapu and Mandaue (UCLM) Student Supreme Council, proudly serves as the official student publication of UCLM Senior High School. This initiative was envisioned as a dynamic platform to amplify the voices of students, showcase their creative talents, and promote active participation within the school community.

2024

Ang Silakbo proudly marked a significant milestone in 2024 by participating in its first-ever Division Schools Press Conference, held in the vibrant city of Mandaue. This event provided an incredible opportunity for our team to showcase their journalistic skills, creativity, and dedication on a competitive stage alongside other talented student publications.

2024

The launch of the official Ang Silakbo website marks a significant step in expanding our digital presence and enhancing accessibility to our content. This platform serves as a dynamic hub for news, features, creative works, and updates, bringing the voices and stories of the UCLM Senior High School community to a broader audience.

Future

Ang Silakbo continues to grow, innovate, and inspire the University of Cebu Lapu-Lapu and Mandaue (UCLM) community by serving as a beacon of creativity, journalism, and unity. With each passing year, we strive to enhance our content, expand our reach, and adapt to the evolving needs of our audience.

Our Mentors

Testimonials From Our Advisers

The dedicated mentors guiding our publication

June Marie Basilgo

Ang Silakbo Adviser

"As one of the advisers of Ang Silakbo, I am truly honored and grateful to be part of this passionate team of student journalists. Guiding them has been one of the most fulfilling parts of my journey. It brings me joy to share what I know and see them grow in their craft. This has also reignited the journalist in me, the same fire I had when I was a student. Sometimes, I wish I could have done more: more time, more support, and more resources. But I hope that in the little ways I've helped them, they'll continue to bloom into voices that will move and inspire. This chapter we've shared is one I will always treasure."

Queenie Marie Paquibot

Ang Silakbo Adviser

"It is a great privilege for me to be one of the advisers of Ang Silakbo because it is an avenue for students to hone their skills in journalism, whether through writing, editing, layouting, broadcasting or anything related to it. Through the publication, they not only practice their craft, but they also have an opportunity to grow in their character as they learn to communicate if they can commit, embrace responsibilities as they try to balance academic pressure at the same time and apply integrity in everything they do. Ang Silakbo will always hold a dear place in my heart because, as I guide them along the way, they also teach me in many ways.

Diana Rose Alba

Ang Silakbo Adviser

"Ako'y lubos na nagpapasalamat na mabigyan ng pagkakataon maging tagapayo ng ating pamahayagan. Isang malaking karangalan ang maging bahagi ng isang pamilya na puno ng mga masisigasig, malilikhain, talentado, matatalino, at mapanuring mag-aaral at kapwa tagapayo. Hindi naging madali ang taon na ito para sa ating lahat. Maraming tayong tinahak na mga pagsubok at mga "first time." Hindi lahat ng plano at ekspektasyon ay nakamit at napagtagumpayan, ngunit ang lahat ng ito ay hinarap natin nang sama-sama, na may ngiti at pananabik. Maraming salamat sa tiwala at sa pagkakataong makilala kayo, Kasilakbo. Ang makapagbahagi ng kaalaman, at matuto mula sa inyo ay isang pribilehiyo at karanansang hinding hindi ko malilimutan. Kayo ang kumumpleto ng taon ko, Kasilakbo!"

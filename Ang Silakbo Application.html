About Ang Silakbo

Ang Silakbo is the official student publication of the University of Cebu Lapu-Lapu and Mandaue (UCLM) Senior High School department. Established in 2021, we aim to create awareness and inform the public about student activities, policies, current issues, and achievements.

Our dedicated team of writers, editors, photographers, and designers work together to deliver high-quality content that matters to our school community.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about joining Ang Silakbo.

What is Ang Silakbo?
Ang Silakbo is the official student publication of the University of Cebu Lapu-Lapu and Mandaue (UCLM) Senior High School department. Established in 2021, the publication aims to create awareness and inform the public about the actions of the student body, policies, current issues, students' welfare, programs, official activities, as well as the SHS department's achievements.
Who can apply to join Ang Silakbo?
We welcome applications from students who are enrolled in University of Cebu Lapu-Lapu and Mandaue Senior High School Department, that are passionate about journalism, writing, photography, videography, and other media-related fields. Whether you're a beginner or have prior experience, we encourage you to apply.
What positions are available?
We offer a wide range of positions for individuals passionate about journalism, media, and creative storytelling. These include Feature News Editor, Editorial Editor, Local News Editor, Sports News Editor, and Entertainment News Writer, each responsible for covering different aspects of news and storytelling. For those interested in creative and visual content, we have roles such as Literary Writer, Layout Artist, Editorial Cartoonist, Photojournalist, Videographer, and Video Editor. Additionally, aspiring broadcasters can join our team to deliver engaging and informative content. Whether your strength lies in writing, design, photography, videography, or broadcasting, there's a position suited for you.
How can I apply?
To apply, simply click on any "Apply Now" button on this page to open our application form. Complete all required fields with accurate information about your experience, skills, and the specific position you're interested in. If you need to pause your application, you can close the form and return later - we'll save your progress! Once submitted, our team will review your application and contact you if your qualifications align with our needs.
What happens after I submit my application?
After you submit your application, our team will carefully review your qualifications and experience to determine if they align with our requirements. If your profile is a good fit for the position, we will reach out to you to schedule an interview and discuss the next steps in the selection process. Be sure to check your email regularly for updates regarding your application status, as all important notifications and further instructions will be sent there.

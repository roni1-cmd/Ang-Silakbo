Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about working with our media team

How do I select the right team members for my project?
Browse our team members by category or use the search function to find specialists with the skills you need. You can add members to your selection and then contact us to discuss your project requirements. Our team will help match you with the perfect professionals for your specific needs.
What types of projects does the SILAKBO Media Team handle?
Our team handles a wide range of media projects including but not limited to: editorial content creation, news coverage, feature writing, photography, videography, broadcasting, layout design, graphic design, social media management, and multimedia production. We can tailor our services to meet the specific requirements of your project.
How much does it cost to hire team members?
Pricing varies depending on the scope of your project, the number of team members required, and the duration of the engagement. We offer flexible pricing options including project-based fees, hourly rates, and retainer arrangements. Contact us with your project details for a customized quote.
How quickly can the team start working on my project?
Our team can typically begin work within 1-2 weeks of project approval, depending on current workload and the specific team members required. For urgent projects, we may be able to accommodate faster turnaround times. We'll provide a clear timeline during our initial consultation.
Do you offer remote collaboration options?
Yes, we are fully equipped for remote collaboration. Our team uses professional project management and communication tools to ensure seamless workflow regardless of location. We can adapt to your preferred collaboration platforms or use our own established systems.

ANG SILAKBO Admin Help Center

Managing Submissions

As an admin, you can review, approve, reject, or delete submissions from users. This guide will help you navigate the submission management process effectively.

  • View Submission Details: Click on the "View" button to see the full submission, including content, author information, and any attached media.
  • Approve Submissions: After reviewing, click "Approve" to publish the submission on the platform. Approved submissions will be visible to all users.
  • Reject Submissions: If a submission doesn't meet your guidelines, click "Reject". You may want to provide feedback to the author about why their submission was rejected.
  • Delete Submissions: For submissions that violate terms or are spam, use the "Delete" option. This action is permanent and cannot be undone.
Pro Tip

Review submissions regularly to maintain a healthy content flow. We recommend checking for new submissions at least once daily to avoid backlogs.

Important

Always review the full content before approving. Check for inappropriate content, plagiarism, or other policy violations that might have been missed by automated filters.

Filtering and Sorting

Efficiently manage submissions with these powerful filtering and sorting tools:

  • Search: Use the search box to find submissions by title, author name, or content. The search is case-insensitive and matches partial text.
  • Sort Options: Arrange submissions by newest first (default), oldest first, or alphabetically by title.
  • Filter by Status: Quickly filter to see only pending, approved, or rejected submissions.
  • Pagination: Navigate through multiple pages of submissions using the pagination controls at the bottom.
Note

Filters are applied in real-time and will persist until you change them or refresh the page. Use the "Reset Filters" button if you get no results and want to start over.

Exporting Data

You can export submission data for record-keeping, analysis, or backup purposes:

  • Export All or Filtered: The export function will export either all submissions or just the currently filtered set, depending on your active filters.
  • CSV Format: Data is exported in CSV format, compatible with Excel, Google Sheets, and other spreadsheet applications.
  • Included Fields: The export includes ID, title, author name, email, content, status, and creation date for each submission.
  • File Naming: Exported files are named "silakbo-submissions-[current-date].csv" for easy identification.
Data Privacy Warning

Exported data may contain sensitive personal information. Always handle exported files according to your organization's data privacy policies and applicable regulations.

Understanding Statistics

The dashboard provides real-time statistics to help you monitor submission activity:

  • Total Submissions: The overall count of all submissions received.
  • Pending Review: Submissions awaiting your review and decision.
  • Approved: Submissions you've approved for publication.
  • Rejected: Submissions you've rejected.
Monitoring Trends

Watch for unusual spikes in pending submissions or rejection rates, as these might indicate issues with submission quality or potential spam attacks.

Admin Best Practices

Follow these guidelines to maintain an effective and fair submission review process:

  • Consistency: Apply your content guidelines consistently across all submissions.
  • Timeliness: Try to review submissions within 48 hours to keep creators engaged.
  • Documentation: Keep records of why submissions were rejected, especially for borderline cases.
  • Feedback: When possible, provide constructive feedback to help creators improve future submissions.
  • Regular Backups: Export submission data periodically as a backup measure.

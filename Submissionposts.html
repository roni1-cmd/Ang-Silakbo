Submission Process Overview

Submitting your work to Ang Silakbo is a simple process that involves four main steps:

  1. Author Information: Provide your name and contact details.
  2. Content Creation: Write or paste your literary work into our editor.
  3. Media Upload: Add a featured image that represents your work (optional but recommended).
  4. Review & Submit: Check your submission and send it to our editorial team.

After submission, our editorial team will review your work and get back to you within 3-4 class days.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Fill in Author Information

Enter your full name as you want it to appear with your published work. Provide a valid email address where we can contact you regarding your submission.

Pro Tip

Consider using a pen name if you prefer to publish under a pseudonym. Just make sure to provide your real email address for communication purposes.

Step 2: Create Your Content

Give your work a compelling title. Use our rich text editor to write or paste your content. The editor allows you to format your text with bold, italic, headings, and more.

Pro Tip

If you're pasting content from another document, use the "Paste as plain text" option (Ctrl+Shift+V) to avoid formatting issues. This ensures your work maintains consistent styling.

Step 3: Upload Media

Click the "Upload Image" button to add a visual element to your submission. You can upload images from your device, Google Drive, or other sources. The ideal image should represent the theme or mood of your work.

Pro Tip

Use high-quality images with a 16:9 aspect ratio for the best display on our platform. Make sure you have the rights to use any image you upload. Free stock photo sites like Unsplash or Pexels are great resources.

Step 4: Review and Submit

Add any additional notes or context about your work in the "Additional Notes" field. Review all your information carefully before clicking the "Submit Work" button.

Pro Tip

Save your work as a draft if you need to come back to it later. Your draft will be available in the "Drafts" section of your dashboard for up to 30 days.

After Submission

Once you submit your work, you'll receive:

  • An immediate confirmation on screen
  • A confirmation email with your submission details
  • A unique submission ID for tracking purposes

You can view your submission status at any time by logging into your account and checking the "Submissions" section of your dashboard.

Our editorial team typically reviews submissions within 7-10 business days. You will be notified via email when your submission status changes.

Content Guidelines

Ang Silakbo accepts a wide range of literary works, including:

  • Poetry: Up to 5 poems per submission, maximum 50 lines each
  • Short Stories: 1,000-5,000 words
  • Flash Fiction: Up to 1,000 words
  • Essays: 1,000-3,000 words
  • Literary Criticism: 1,500-4,000 words
  • Visual Art: Photography, digital art, illustrations (with artist statement)

All submissions must be original work that has not been previously published elsewhere (including personal blogs and social media).

Important Note

We particularly encourage works that explore Filipino identity, culture, and experiences, though submissions on all topics are welcome.

Formatting Requirements

  • Use standard fonts (our editor defaults to an easy-to-read font)
  • Double-space prose submissions
  • Use single spacing for poetry
  • Include paragraph breaks where appropriate
  • Avoid excessive formatting (such as colored text or unusual fonts)
  • For poetry with specific formatting needs, please note this in the "Additional Notes" section

Our editorial team may make minor formatting adjustments to ensure consistency across our platform, but we will always respect the integrity of your work.

Content Restrictions

Ang Silakbo does not accept content that:

  • Promotes hate speech or discrimination
  • Contains explicit or gratuitous violence
  • Includes pornographic material
  • Violates copyright or intellectual property rights
  • Promotes illegal activities
  • Contains defamatory content about real people

We reserve the right to reject any submission that does not align with our community values.

Sensitive Content

If your work deals with sensitive topics such as mental health, trauma, or political issues, please include a content warning in your "Additional Notes" section.

Image Guidelines

If you choose to include images with your submission:

  • Images should be high-resolution (at least 1200px wide)
  • Acceptable formats: JPG, PNG, BMP, JPEG and GIF
  • Maximum file size: 5MB
  • You must have the rights to use and publish the image
  • Images should be relevant to your written content
  • Avoid images with text overlays or watermarks
Important Note

If you don't own the image, make sure it's licensed for use (e.g., Creative Commons) or in the public domain. Always provide attribution when required.

General Questions

Q: Is there a fee to submit work to Ang Silakbo?

A: No, submissions to Ang Silakbo are completely free. We believe in providing an accessible platform for all writers and artists.

Q: How long does the review process take?

A: Our editorial team typically reviews submissions within 7-10 business days. You will receive an email notification when your submission status changes.

Q: Can I submit multiple works at once?

A: Yes, you can submit up to 5 poems in a single poetry submission. For prose works, we recommend submitting one piece at a time to allow our editors to give each work proper attention.

Q: What happens if my work is accepted?

A: If your work is accepted, you will receive an acceptance email with further instructions. Your work will be published on our platform according to our publishing schedule, and you'll receive a link to share with your network.

Q: Can I submit work in languages other than English?

A: Yes, we accept submissions in Filipino/Tagalog and other Philippine languages. We encourage works that showcase our linguistic diversity. Please provide an English translation if possible.

Technical Questions

Q: Can I save my submission as a draft and come back to it later?

A: Yes, you can save your work as a draft by clicking the "Save Draft" button at the bottom of the form. Your drafts will be available in the "Drafts" section of your dashboard for up to 30 days.

Q: I'm having trouble uploading images. What should I do?

A: Make sure your image meets our guidelines (under 5MB, JPG/PNG/GIF format). If you continue to have issues, try using a different browser or device, or contact our support team with details about the error you're experiencing.

Q: Can I edit my submission after it's been submitted?

A: You can make minor edits to your submission while it's still in the "Under Review" status. Once it's been accepted or published, you'll need to contact our editorial team for any changes.

Q: Is my personal information secure?

A: Yes, we take data privacy seriously. Your personal information is encrypted and stored securely. We never share your contact information with third parties without your consent. Please see our Privacy Policy for more details.

Rights and Publication

Q: Who retains the rights to my work?

A: You retain all rights to your work. Ang Silakbo requests non-exclusive rights to publish your work on our platform and in potential anthologies or collections. This means you're free to publish your work elsewhere as well.

Q: Can I publish my work elsewhere after it appears on Ang Silakbo?

A: Yes, you are free to publish your work elsewhere after it appears on Ang Silakbo. We simply ask that you acknowledge that it was first published with us when submitting to other publications.

Q: Will I be compensated for my work?

A: Currently, we are unable to offer monetary compensation for published works. However, we provide exposure to our growing audience and include author profiles to help you build your portfolio.

Q: Can I remove my work from your platform after it's been published?

A: Yes, you can request to have your work removed at any time by contacting our editorial team. Please note that it may take up to 7 business days to process your request.

Report an Issue

If you encounter technical issues with our submission platform, please provide the following information when contacting us:

  • Your browser and version
  • Your device type (desktop, tablet, mobile)
  • A detailed description of the issue
  • Screenshots of any error messages (if possible)
  • Steps to reproduce the issue

This information will help us resolve your issue more quickly. You can send technical issue reports to: support@angsilakbo.com

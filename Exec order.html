University Publication Charter

University Publication Charter

Establishing the framework for student journalism and expression at the University of Cebu Lapu-Lapu and Mandaue

Executive Order No. 211

Preamble

Effective Date: Immediately upon ratification

WHEREAS

  • 1
    The Constitution mandates the SSC President to implement programs and projects that will benefit the student body and promote student welfare and development;
  • 2
    The SSC Presidential authority includes the creation of Ad Hoc Committees to address specific needs and concerns of the student community;
  • 3
    Students have the right to responsible freedom of speech and expression, which is essential for their intellectual and personal growth;
  • 4
    There is a need for student awareness regarding university policies, achievements, and activities to foster a more engaged and informed student community;
  • 5
    The SSC has a responsibility to address student concerns and provide a platform for student voices to be heard and acknowledged;
  • 6
    An independent student publication is required to serve as a medium for student expression, information dissemination, and community building.

Operational Protocols

Ethical Journalism Standards

  • Rigorous fact-checking and verification process for all published content
  • Protection of source confidentiality and journalistic integrity
  • Mandatory disclosure of potential conflicts of interest
  • Comprehensive plagiarism prevention and content originality system
  • Balanced reporting with fair representation of all perspectives

Publication Timeline

  • Weekly digital content updates on the official website
  • Monthly digital newsletter distributed via email
  • Quarterly print edition with comprehensive coverage
  • Annual compendium publication highlighting the year's best content
  • Special editions for major university events and milestones

Content Guidelines

  • Focus on campus news, events, and student achievements
  • Coverage of relevant local and national issues affecting students
  • Feature stories highlighting student and faculty accomplishments
  • Opinion pieces representing diverse student perspectives
  • Creative works including poetry, short stories, and artwork

Publication Timeline

January 2021

Concept Development

Initial brainstorming sessions and planning for the publication structure, including defining the vision, mission, and core values of Ang Silakbo.

March 2021

Team Formation

Recruitment and organization of the editorial team through a rigorous selection process to identify talented student journalists and creative professionals.

June 2021

Charter Ratification

Official approval and ratification of the publication charter by the Student Supreme Council and university administration.

September 2021

Training Program

Comprehensive training program for all staff members covering journalistic ethics, writing techniques, design principles, and digital publishing.

January 2022

First Edition Release

Launch of the inaugural issue of Ang Silakbo, featuring campus news, student achievements, and creative works from the university community.

March 2022

Digital Expansion

Introduction of online platforms including a dedicated website, social media channels, and digital newsletter to reach a broader audience.
